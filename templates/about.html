California Wildfires & Machine Learning

Why It Matters.

In 2021, California wildfires have burned more than 1 million acres and destroyed over 3,600 buldings. As climate change continues to worsen, the threat wildfires pose to California will only become greater. It is important now, more than ever, to not only monitor these wildfires but also determine ways to mitigate climate change and then negative impact it has on our planet.


Our Goal.

We created this dashboard as a way to track active wildfires, view historical wildfire data, and get updates for developing wildfire situations.
